# How Your Finger Unlocks Your Phone (And Why It’s Kind of Insane)

![Cover](assets/cover.png)

---

## Imagine this.

You pick up your phone.  
Touch it for less than a second.  
And boom — it unlocks.

> Your phone just recognized you… using your skin.

---

## 🧠 What’s really happening?

Your fingerprint is a unique pattern of ridges and valleys.

Think of it like a map of your finger.

---

## ⚙️ Step 1: Scanning your finger

### 📸 Optical Sensor
![Optical](assets/optical.png)

Takes an image using light.

---

### ⚡ Capacitive Sensor
![Capacitive](assets/capacitive.png)

Uses electrical signals.

---

### 🔊 Ultrasonic Sensor
![Ultrasonic](assets/ultrasonic.png)

Uses sound waves to create 3D scan.

---

## 🧬 Step 2: Turning your finger into data

Phone extracts key points and converts into data.

---

## 🔐 Step 3: Matching

New scan is compared with stored data.

If match → Unlock  
Else → Try again  

---

## 🎯 Final Thought

All this happens in less than a second.
